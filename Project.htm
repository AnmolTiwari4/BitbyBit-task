Our Events

VIT Bhopal's Bit by Bit club organizes a wide array of events that cater to diverse interests, from technical workshops to hackathons. These events provide students with opportunities to showcase their talents, learn new skills, and network with professionals and peers. Annual events such as VIT Hack and VIT Fest are particularly popular, drawing participants from across the country. The vibrant campus life is enriched with guest lectures, seminars, and social initiatives, making VIT Bhopal a dynamic environment for both academic and personal growth.

Our Motive

Our main aim is of sharing the knowledge about computer science engineering, which includes sharing of knowledge of various domains including cyber security, blockchain, and various domains of meachine learning

This knowledge sharing allows members to improve their skills and knowledge in various fields.
